What is FLEXIDOTS?

FLEXIDOTS is a modular electronic kit specifically designed for wearable prototyping scenarios, which is addressing the challenge of making ergonomic, simplistic, and durable wearable electronics. This solution contains adaptive body attachments, optimized-wiring microcontroller & sensors kit, and software packages. Read the full story here.


Why you need FLEXIDOTS?

If you need to quickly prototype wearable electronics without designing complicated wearable structures or stitching conductive wires, FLEXIDOTS is a good starting point. You can use various modules to build your electronic system simply plug and play. Also you can use modular body attachments with different shapes and sizes to create your own wearable structure.

FLEXIDOTS is not just a hardware system, it also provides a variety of interactive examples that facilitate communication between the hardware and software. If you are planning to develop a custom controller for your digital experience, FLEXIDOTS is here to help you validate your idea!
