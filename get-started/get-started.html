Let's Get Started!

This page will walk you through the basic moduals of FLEXIDOTS, and help you create your first wearable experience!


Anatomy of Flexidots

FLEXIDOTS is structured into several basic modules: Band, Connectors, Microcontroller, Input, Output, Wire, and Power. Each of these parts is color-coded for better arrangement and identification. You can find the full list of components on Hardware page.

The Microcontroller, Input, and Output modules can be assembled with snaps system on any part of the band. Different shapes of connectors and bands can be combined to fit various parts of the body, or just let your imagination run wild!

Various types of components can be adapted to FLEXIDOTS system. In this Get Started stutorial, we have following examples for different levels of usage: Arduino / Arduino & p5.js / micro:bit & Scratch (Comming Soon) / ESP32 & Unity (Comming Soon)
